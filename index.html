Privacy Policy for PixaPulse

Last Updated: July 8, 2025

PixaPulse ("the App") is a mobile application that uses Bluetooth technology to provide interactive experiences. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and disclose user and device data when you use the App. By using PixaPulse, you agree to the practices outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to enable the App’s functionality:

  • Bluetooth Data: The App requires Bluetooth to detect broadcasted BLE beacon signals from event infrastructure, enabling synchronized light and vibration effects during live shows.
  • Location Data: Location is used only to verify if you are within the bounds of a supported stadium or venue. This data is not tracked or stored.
  • Device-Related Data: The App may access basic device information or permissions, such as those needed for Bluetooth communication, to support its core features.
  • Processing: All data is processed temporarily in memory during use and discarded immediately after.
  • Storage and Sharing: No data is stored on the device or sent to the developer or third parties.
  • Other Data: The App does not collect personal information like names, emails, or user-generated content, and no user accounts are required.

2. How We Use Your Information

Collected data is used only to make the App work as intended, enabling Bluetooth-based interactions. It is not used for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The App does not share any data with third parties, either on or off the device. All processing happens locally on your device.

4. Data Security

Data is protected during use with standard security measures and is not stored after the App’s operation, reducing any risk of unauthorized access.

5. User Control and Data Deletion

  • Control: Some data collection is required for the App to function and cannot be turned off without limiting features. You can manage permissions through your device settings.
  • Deletion: Since data is only used temporarily and not stored, there is no data to delete. The App does not use user accounts.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not designed for children under 13. It complies with applicable privacy laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party tools to support its functionality, such as Bluetooth communication. These tools do not collect or share data beyond what is needed for the App to work.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. Updates will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last Updated" date.
